About the Archive

Welcome to the Bulgarian Heritage Archive, a digital preservation system dedicated to cataloging and maintaining the rich historical artifacts of Bulgaria's ancient past.

Our Mission

From the golden treasures of the Thracians to the intricate relics of the Byzantine era, our goal is to provide curators, historians, and enthusiasts with a robust platform to track, study, and preserve these priceless artifacts.

System Features

  • Comprehensive Cataloging: Track artifact origins, materials, and historical significance.
  • Preservation Logs: Monitor the condition and restoration history of each item over time.
  • Dynamic Filtering: Easily search through periods, materials, and names to locate specific records.
